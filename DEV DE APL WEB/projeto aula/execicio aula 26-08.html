Customs Declaration

19 CFR 122.27, 148.12, 148.13, 148.110,148.111, 1498; 31 CFR 5316

Each arriving traveler or responsible family member must provide the following information (only ONE written declaration per family is required). The term “family” is defined as “members of a family residing in the same household who are related by blood, marriage, domestic relationship, or adoption.”

1 Family Name

First (Given) Middle

2 Birth date Month Day Year

3 Number of Family members traveling with you

4 (a) U.S. Street Address (hotel name/destination (b) City(c) State

5 (a) U.S. Street Address (hotel name/destination (b) City(c) State

6 (a) U.S. Street Address (hotel name/destination (b) City(c) State

7 (a) U.S. Street Address (hotel name/destination (b) City(c) State
